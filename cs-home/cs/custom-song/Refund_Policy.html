Welcome to M7 Corporation's Privacy Policy

At M7 Corporation, we pride ourselves on delivering high-quality, personalized songs that meet the unique needs and expectations of our clients. Due to the custom nature of our services, the following refund policy applies to all transactions on our platform.

Refund Eligibility

  1. Pre-Production Cancellation: If you decide to cancel your order before we begin production, you may be eligible for a full or partial refund depending on the stage of the order preparation. Please contact us immediately if you wish to cancel your order.
  2. Post-Production Cancellation: Once production has commenced, we generally cannot offer a refund as resources and time have been invested in creating your custom song.
  3. Non-Delivery: If for some reason you do not receive your song within the agreed delivery time frame due to an error on our part, you are entitled to a refund. Please reach out to us if your delivery is delayed beyond the expected timeline.
  4. Major Defects: Although all songs go through quality control, unexpected errors can occur. If the final product has significant discrepancies from your original instructions and we are unable to correct these in a revised version, a partial refund may be considered.
  5. Satisfaction Guarantee: While we strive for your complete satisfaction, we cannot offer refunds based on personal taste or a change of mind after the song's delivery.

Process for Requesting a Refund

To initiate a refund, please contact our customer support team via email at [Your Support Email] with your order number and a detailed explanation of your issue. We aim to respond to all queries within 48 hours.

Exceptions

Please note that special offers, discounts, and additional service features may have different refund terms, which will be clearly communicated at the time of purchase.
